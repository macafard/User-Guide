Getting started

This user guide describes step by step how to add, edit and delete new headlines and reports, and how to check any headlines created and saved on the Metcore platform. You can check the content section on the previous page for further details.

To get started with Metcore, log in to the platform with your account. Once you log in, the screen will show you the platform interface.

  1. Profile/Account area: On the upper-right side of the window, you will find a section with your profile name and photo. If you click the photo or name, you will have access to a variety of options and settings for your account and platform.
  2. Navigation bar: In this bar, on the upper side of the window, you will find six different tabs: Dashboard, Start a claim, Find a claim, Administration, Reporting and Form. Each one holds sections to navigate through, as shown on the Dashboard.
  3. Headlines: The headlines recently created by you or other contributors are displayed here, as well as the Email Directory and Office Directory sections on the upper-left side.

After getting familiar with the interface, you can go and check the Headlines section.

How to access to your headlines

On the navigation bar, go to the Dashboard tab and click All Headlines. You will see a new section on your screen where you will be able to check the headlines you created by date, with their titles, authors, and descriptions.

In this section you have three options to manage your headlines: Add New Headline, Edit and Delete.

Adding, editing, and deleting headlines

  • To add a new headline:

    Click Add New Headline on the upper-left side, next to All News Headlines. A new screen will be displayed with a title and a description box to complete.

  • To edit a headline:

    Click Edit on the right side, next to the headline you want to edit. A new screen will be displayed with the headline selected to edit.

    Remember to save your changes when you finish editing the headline. The screen will show a message confirming the headline update.

  • To delete a headline:

    Click Delete on the right side, next to Edit. A dialog box will be opened to confirm the action.

Checking headlines

If you want to go back and check your headlines after editing or creating one, click the METCORE name, go to the Dashboard again and click All Headlines. Now you will see the headlines you created or edited first.
